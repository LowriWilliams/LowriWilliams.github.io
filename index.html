About Me

I am currently a research data scientist as part of the Data Innovation Accelerator at the School of Computer Science & Informatics, Cardiff University.

Before this post, I was a research assistant as part of the National Corpus of Contemporary Welsh project at the School of English, Communication and Philosophy, Cardiff University.

The main focus of my work revolves around machine learning, particularly natural language processing, data mining, text classification and sentiment analysis. Such expertise and knowledge is transferable to several other research domains. In particular, I have formed collaborations with multidisciplinary experts from research fields such as cybersecurity to present research outputs and findings within several reputable academic journals.
